Anastesiac Simulation Room

For the course Immersive Technologies and Storytelling (IMTS) I worked as a programmer in a team on an assignment from the Leiden University Medical Center. For this assignment we were asked to create a realistic operating room where instructors could put a variety of situations before anaesthesiologists and respond to how they would handle the situation. In this project my main responsibilities were networking data between trainer and trainee, implementing VR interactions for the player to use the computer in the room, and helping with the networking between trainees .
